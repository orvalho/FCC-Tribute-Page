Persistence

a prime ingredient in success

Persistence refers to perseverance in spite of fatigue or frustration. It's the willingness to keep going even when the odds are bad and our enthusiasm has waned. Persistence is a prime ingredient in success in many pursuits such as athletics, academics, business, etc. Woody Allen once said that 80% of success is just showing up. It is not thinking or feeling but action that really counts.
